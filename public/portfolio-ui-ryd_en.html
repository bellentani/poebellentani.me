RYD Agro

RYD is a startup from Belém/PA that operates in auctions of offers to buy agricultural commodities. I did the Art Direction for logo development and then wireframes and navigational flow, delivering a navigable prototype to be implemented by the development team, along with a UI KIT based on Google Material.
